General Surgery at Sri Mahalakshmi Hospital

Sri Mahalakshmi Hospital provides expert care in General Surgery, offering a full spectrum of surgical procedures with a focus on safety, precision, and patient comfort. Our experienced surgical team ensures that each patient receives comprehensive pre-operative and post-operative care.

Our General Surgery Services Include:
  • Appendectomy (Appendix Removal)
  • Hernia Repair (Inguinal, Umbilical, Incisional)
  • Gallbladder Removal (Cholecystectomy)
  • Abscess Drainage & Minor Surgeries
  • Piles, Fissure & Fistula Treatment
  • Lump, Cyst & Lipoma Excision
  • Biopsy and Diagnostic Surgical Procedures

Our surgical department uses advanced technology and minimally invasive techniques where possible to reduce recovery time and improve outcomes. We are committed to delivering high-quality surgical care in a compassionate and professional setting.

From initial consultation to recovery, our general surgeons maintain clear communication and support throughout your journey. We focus on early diagnosis, patient education, and tailored surgical plans. By combining experience with innovation, we help patients return to their daily lives quickly and safely. Each surgery is handled with the utmost precision, and we maintain a sterile environment to minimize complications.

Post-surgery rehabilitation and care are equally vital to ensure smooth recovery. At Sri Mahalakshmi Hospital, we monitor your progress through follow-up consultations and provide pain management, dietary guidance, and wound care instructions. Our friendly staff ensures you're comfortable, informed, and on the right path to healing. We strive to make every patient’s surgical experience stress-free and successful.

Common Symptoms Requiring Surgical Attention:
  • Persistent abdominal pain or bloating
  • Lumps or swelling in any part of the body
  • Unhealed wounds or abscesses
  • Blood in stool or chronic constipation
  • Sudden vomiting or fever with pain
  • Painful hernias or bulges
  • Recurring anal bleeding or discomfort
Precautions & Recovery Tips After Surgery:
  • Follow post-operative instructions strictly
  • Keep surgical wounds clean and dry
  • Avoid lifting heavy objects until cleared
  • Take medications as prescribed, especially antibiotics
  • Watch for signs of infection or swelling
  • Eat soft, nutritious food for easy digestion
  • Attend all follow-up appointments regularly

Frequently Asked Questions

You May Ask

What types of surgeries do you perform?
We offer a wide range of general surgeries including hernia repair, appendectomy, gallbladder surgery, and minor soft tissue procedures.
Do you offer laparoscopic (keyhole) surgeries?
Yes, we specialize in minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures that reduce pain and promote faster recovery.
What should I expect during a surgical consultation?
During consultation, our surgeon will evaluate your condition, review diagnostic tests, and explain the procedure, risks, and recovery process.
How long is the recovery after surgery?
Recovery time varies based on the surgery but our team ensures personalized post-operative care for a smooth and quick recovery.
Who performs surgeries at Sri Mahalakshmi Hospital?
Our qualified and experienced general surgeons ensure safe, effective, and compassionate surgical care tailored to each patient.
