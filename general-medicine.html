Common Symptoms to Watch For:
  • Persistent fever or fatigue
  • Unexplained weight loss or gain
  • Shortness of breath or chest discomfort
  • Digestive issues like acidity, bloating, or diarrhea
  • High blood pressure or blood sugar levels
  • Frequent headaches or dizziness
  • Joint pain or body aches
Precautions & Wellness Tips:
  • Follow a balanced diet low in sugar and salt
  • Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes a day
  • Get adequate sleep and manage stress levels
  • Schedule regular health checkups and screenings
  • Avoid self-medication and consult a doctor for symptoms
  • Maintain hydration and proper hygiene practices
  • Limit alcohol consumption and avoid tobacco use
